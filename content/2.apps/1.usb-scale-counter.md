## Overview

USB Scale Counter is a Windows desktop application that automates piece counting using a **Dymo M10 USB postal scale**. Instead of manually counting identical items, simply place them on the scale and let the app calculate the quantity based on weight.

## How It Works

1. **Configure** the expected weight of a single item and the target quantity
2. **Place items** on the connected Dymo M10 USB scale
3. **Read the result** — the app calculates the count and provides instant color-coded feedback:
   - **Orange** — fewer items than required
   - **Green** — exact target count reached
   - **Red** — too many items on the scale
